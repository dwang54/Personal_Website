Lunar Lander

Developed and integrated SystemVerilog program onto the ICE40HX-8K FPGA that accepts real-time user adjustments to engine thrust to achieve a safe landing in simulations. Functionality includes dynamic LED indicators of lunar lander's condition and ability to toggle between different displays of aerospace relevant values.

SystemVerilog, Virtuoso, RTL Design, FPGA

Audio Equalizer

Designed and built an audio equalizer circuit that allows for adjustment of volume across various frequencies. Achieved power output >400 mW across a frequency range from 200 Hz - 10 kHz. Implementation involved low-pass, high-pass, and band pass filters alongside inverting and noninverting op amps.

Audio Engineering, Circuit Design, LTSpice
